## Release-content requirements

For each published article or training asset, confirm:

- Feature is available in production/staging as documented.
- Screens and labels match current UI.
- Role and permission prerequisites are correct.
- Steps include common errors and escalation path.
- Sensitive financial controls are described without exposing privileged bypass procedures.
- Article owner, review date, and related release version are recorded.
