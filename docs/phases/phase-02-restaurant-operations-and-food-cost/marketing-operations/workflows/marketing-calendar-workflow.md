# Marketing Calendar Workflow

## Purpose

Give Marketing a trusted single view of planned, scheduled, live, and completed campaigns, promotions, launches, content, branch openings, mall events, and seasonal dates.

## Calendar entries

- Campaign dates
- Promotion validity dates
- New-item launch/soft-launch/full-launch dates
- Content production and publication dates
- Creative approval deadlines
- Branch rollout dates
- Branch-opening campaign milestones
- Mall event dates
- Holiday/seasonal planning dates

## Workflow

1. Authorized user creates/updates campaign, promotion, launch, creative item, or explicit marketing event.
2. System creates/updates the authorized calendar representation.
3. User filters by brand, branch, campaign, owner/assignee, status, channel, and date range.
4. User opens event to reach the authoritative source record.
5. Date changes validate permissions and required campaign/promotion state rules.
6. System records activity and sends configured reminders.

## Conflict handling

- Calendar can display overlap; it does not automatically prevent concurrent campaigns unless a configured policy requires conflict review.
- Potential branch, date, channel, or promotion conflict should be visible through filters/alerts and routed to configured owner.
- Date-only promotion validity must not shift across timezones.

## Publishing boundary

The calendar is planning/execution control. It does not automatically publish social content, change POS pricing, release ad spend, or enable a discount without separate approved integration/workflow.
