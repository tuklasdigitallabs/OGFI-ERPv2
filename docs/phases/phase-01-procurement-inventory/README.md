# Phase I — Procurement and Inventory Control

**Status:** Build-ready documentation baseline  
**Goal:** Replace manual purchasing, receiving, stock movement, transfer, count, wastage, and approval workflows with auditable digital controls.

## In Scope

- User, role, location-scope and approval foundations
- Supplier and item master data
- Purchase Requests, quotation comparison, Purchase Orders and emergency purchase controls
- Receiving Reports and delivery discrepancies
- Warehouse-to-branch and branch-to-branch transfers
- Inventory ledger, stock on hand, counts, variance, wastage and approved adjustments
- Phase I dashboards, reports, notifications, audit logs and attachments

## Start With

1. `implementation/PHASE1_TECHNICAL_BUILD_PLAN.md`
2. `implementation/PHASE1_BUILD_BACKLOG_AND_ACCEPTANCE_CRITERIA.md`
3. Applicable workflow under `workflows/`
4. Applicable screen specification under `specs/`
5. Core data, roles, approvals and security standards

## Phase I Readiness Rule

Do not call a Phase I module complete until it passes its workflow acceptance criteria, role/scope tests, ledger integrity tests, audit tests, and relevant mobile/tablet checks.
